Meet your new Communications assistant

ITO helps you make voice and video connections from popular text messaging platforms.

means "thread, yarn, or string" in japanese. We like to think of It as an

assistant that makes connections.

Just like string between two cans.

How it works

Give Commands and it will help you connect with others.

Sarah Finkle

3:01 PM

/ito @johnyaya

ITO will call you and slack user “johnyaya” and connect the both of you.

Ricky Ricardo

3:52 PM

/ito 14075552654 @johnwhorfin

Jay Dubb
